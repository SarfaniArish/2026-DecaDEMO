What does “Best By” mean?

Best By

Best By means the product is freshest before this date, but it can still be safe after.

It helps customers judge quality, not automatically safety.

When this is explained clearly, shoppers feel confident buying near-date items.

What does “Sell By” mean?

Sell By

Sell By is mainly for the store. It helps employees rotate items.

Customers can often still use the product after the Sell By date.

This reduces confusion and supports better sell-through of discounted items.

What does “Use By” mean?

Use By

Use By is the recommended last date for best quality.

It’s the strictest of the three because it’s closer to a “final quality window.”

Now the shopper has learned all three labels in under 30 seconds.

Without clarity, the shopper walks away

When labels are confusing, customers skip near-date items. That lowers sell-through and increases shrink.
